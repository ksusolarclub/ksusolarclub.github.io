☼ Solar Tracking Car
Utilized MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) technology to track the position of the sun and to maximize the energy delivery from the solar array to the car’s battery. This allows for efficiency and optimal power, even when light conditions vary.

☼ Solar Powered Speakers
The sun meets sound in this project, as solar energy powers our audio system and allows it to play music from a processor. A mini battery functions as support, while the solar array and charge controller provide the entire system with power, eliminating the need to constantly replace the battery.

Visit our photo gallery for more images.


☼ Solar Plane
Featured on our home page, we designed and created a radio-controlled airplane using a solar power array to provide power to the system.

☼ Madagascar Solar Project
Worked remotely with a middle school in Ampasimbe, Madagascar to design and implement a system to provide solar power to students so they would be able to use laptops in the classroom.

☼ KansaSolaRace Car
Created a competition between Kansas State and Pittsburg State to allow engineering students to work with solar cars, without the excess budgets typical of regular races.
