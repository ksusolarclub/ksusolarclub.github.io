Who Are We?

Our story.

We are KSU Solar, a student-run engineering organization located in the Carl R. Ice College of Engineering of Kansas State University. Some of our recent projects include the designing and construction of a solar powered car, as well as the development of a system to allow laptop usage in a Madagascar middle school via solar power. Some of our projects utilize skills from both electrical and mechanical engineering, such as CAD, soldering, and PCB design.
